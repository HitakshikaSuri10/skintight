About Me

Hi! I'm Hitakshika Suri, a final-year B.Tech Biotechnology student at VIT Vellore. Driven by a strong commitment to making healthcare more accessible and inclusive, I've dedicated myself to building innovative, real-world solutions at the intersection of biotechnology and technology.

This website — from design to development — was created entirely by me, as a self-taught coder and researcher. My motivation stemmed from a desire to democratize access to healthcare tools and resources, especially in underserved communities.

One of my proudest projects is SkinSight, an AI-powered application that helps detect skin-related issues through image analysis. I built this app independently, combining my biotech knowledge with machine learning techniques. It reflects not just my technical capabilities, but also my deep passion for applying science to serve public health.

Looking ahead, I aspire to work on impactful solutions that improve healthcare delivery and touch lives globally — bridging the gap between advanced medical science and the people who need it most.
